## 相对单位

- **百分比**：这些相对单位允许你将大小、尺寸和其他属性定义为父元素的比例。例如，如果你在元素上设置[width: 50%;](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)，它将占据其父容器宽度的一半。
- **[em](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)单位**：这些单位相对于元素的字体大小。如果你在[font-size](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)属性中使用[ems](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)，文本的大小将相对于父元素的字体大小。
- **[rem](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)单位**：这些单位相对于根元素的字体大小，即[html](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)元素。
- **[vh](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)单位**：[vh](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)代表"视口高度"，[1vh](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)等于视口高度的1%。
