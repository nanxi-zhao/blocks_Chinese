## 使用背景和边框

- **[background-size](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)属性**：此属性用于设置元素的背景大小。一些常见值包括[cover](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)（背景图像覆盖整个元素）和[contain](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)（背景图像适合元素内部）。
- **[background-repeat](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)属性**：此属性用于确定背景图像应如何沿水平和垂直轴重复。[background-repeat](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)的默认值是[repeat](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)，意味着图像将在水平和垂直方向上重复。你也可以通过使用[no-repeat](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)属性指定不应重复。
- **[background-position](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)属性**：此属性用于指定背景图像的位置。它可以设置为特定长度、百分比或关键字值如[top](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)、[bottom](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)、[left](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)、[right](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)和[center](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)。
- **[background-attachment](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)属性**：此属性用于指定背景图像是否应随内容滚动或保持固定位置。主要值有[scroll](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)（默认值），背景图像随内容滚动，和[fixed](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)，背景图像在屏幕上保持相同位置。
- **[background-image](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)属性**：此属性用于设置元素的背景图像。你可以同时设置多个背景图像，并使用[url](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)、[radial-gradient](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)或[linear-gradient](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)函数作为值。
- **[background](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)属性**：这是设置所有背景属性的简写属性。以下是设置背景图像并设置为不重复的示例：[background: no-repeat url("example-url-goes-here");](file:///E:/1.work/byy/%E5%9C%A8%E7%BA%BF%E8%AF%BE%E7%A8%8B/blocks/node_modules/@types/node/stream/consumers.d.ts#L11-L11)
- **背景色和前景色的良好对比度**：确保背景色和前景色具有良好的对比度以使文本可读是很重要的。Web内容可访问性指南（WCAG）建议普通文本的最小对比度比率为4.5:1，大文本为3:1。
